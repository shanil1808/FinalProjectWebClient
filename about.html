Our Story

Founded in 2026, SkyEagle Mobile started with a simple goal: to make premium mobile technology accessible to everyone. We don't just sell phones; we provide the tools that keep you connected to what matters most.

Based in Montreal, our team works tirelessly to source the latest innovations from Apple, Samsung, and Google, ensuring our customers always have the "edge" in tech.

To be the most trusted mobile retailer in Canada, known for integrity, innovation, and impeccable service.

Our Mission

To empower our community by providing cutting-edge mobile solutions that enhance daily life and productivity.
